Schule History

Schule offers a rigorous education grounded in shared values – respect, love of learning, integrity, courage and a commitment to pluralism. Students become lifelong learners who make lasting contributions to the world. Our three campuses unite approximately 1,375 students served by 350 faculty and staff, creating a diverse and inclusive community.
We mold students into thoughtful, disciplined, well-rounded adults by providing a multifaceted education that includes.
